Class Rules

- It's mandatory to attend classes, recordings are for revision not for first-time learning.

- Do join live classes from laptop/desktop, not from mobile.

- Starting 10min and ending 10min of every class is for doubt session.

- Feel free to ask tough questions to your instructor. In case you aren't asking questions 
  in Live classes then either you are a champ or you aren't really learning

- Make sure you practice class questions after every class, watching video aren't going 
  to help you to crack interviews.

- Make sure you complete class assignments max by the end of the current week in which the class 
  was conducted.

- Do share your assignment on the channel and seek feedback from your instructor and peers.

- Feel free to post any question on the channel.

- Collaborate with each other by making friends/group/team in order to learn together 
  and grow together at a faster pace.

- Be ready to push yourself to bring out your best version.
            

“Keep your habits right, keep your future bright.”
